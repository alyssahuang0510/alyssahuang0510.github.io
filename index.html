[03]   How I work工作方法

Every decision sits on a traceable line — finding, decision, outcome.

每個設計決策都落在一條可追溯的線上——證據、決策、成果。

01 — Finding

Start from evidence

從證據出發

A usability study, FAE-measured data, a field complaint, a system constraint. I don’t move until the problem is named in numbers or observed behaviour.

一份可用性研究、FAE 量到的數據、一則現場客訴、一個系統限制。在問題能用數字或觀察到的行為說清楚之前，我不動手。

02 — Decision

Change one thing, on purpose

有意識地改一件事

Each design move ties back to a specific finding — and I can say what I traded off for it. Feasibility is part of the call; I read the engineering constraint, not just the mockup.

每個設計動作都連回某個具體發現——而且我說得出為此取捨了什麼。可行性是決策的一部分；我讀的是工程限制，不只是畫面。

03 — Outcome

Measure what moved

量出改變了什麼

Programming time, returns recovered, sources unified, SUS lift. If a change can’t be measured, I say so — and I don’t claim it.

編程時間、挽回的退貨、收斂的來源數、SUS 提升。如果一個改變無法被量測，我會直說——而且不會把它當成果宣稱。

Every case on this site is built this way. You can always trace a shipped outcome back to the finding that justified it — and that traceability is what I bring to a team, not just to my own work.

這個網站上的每個案例都是這樣寫的。你永遠可以把一個已落地的成果，回溯到當初支持它的那個發現——而我帶進團隊的，正是這種可追溯性，不只是我自己的產出。

[04]   About關於

A designer who wrote the code and owned the design — at the same time.

同時寫程式、也完全主導設計的設計師。

I work the UX layer — research, IA, interaction — on the kind of products operators, IT admins, and developers lean on for hours a day. My undergraduate degree is in Information Management; my master’s is in Interaction Design. That dual root is why an engineering ticket and a research finding read to me as the same kind of document.

我做的是 UX 的結構這一層——研究、資訊架構、互動——服務的是產線操作員、IT 管理者與開發者每天一用就好幾個小時的那種產品。我大學念資訊管理，研究所念互動設計；正因為這兩種底子，工程端的需求單和使用者研究發現，在我眼裡是同一種文件。

For six years I owned the UX of an industrial inspection product end-to-end while writing its C# / MFC code — engineering and design were one job, not two phases. My master’s thesis was an AR rehabilitation game in Unity. Code isn’t my main output anymore, but it shapes how I think about feasibility, system constraints, and exactly what to ask engineers for — which is why cross-functional teams move faster with me in the room. This portfolio is hand-built too — six pages of HTML, CSS and JS, no page builder; I direct the design and the code, paired with Claude.

在德律的那六年，我一邊寫 C# / MFC 程式、一邊完整主導這套工業檢測產品的 UX——工程與設計是同一份工作，不是先後兩個階段。碩士論文則用 Unity 做了一款 AR 復健遊戲。寫程式現在已經不是我的主要產出，但它讓我對可行性、系統限制、以及該跟工程師要什麼，都比一般設計師更有判斷——這也是為什麼，有我在的跨職能團隊溝通起來會更快。這個作品集也是我亲手打造的——六頁 HTML、CSS 與 JS，沒有用 Webflow、Framer 這類拼裝工具；設計與程式都由我主導，搭配 Claude 協作。

The tools I gravitate toward are unglamorous: dense data, expert users, long task sessions, conflicting modes. What I enjoy most is finding the rules buried in that mess — and being the one who gets to define them. My job is to make those legible — and increasingly, to build the tools my own design team plans with.

我喜歡做的工具都不華麗：資料密度高、使用者是專家、一用就是好幾個小時、各種模式還會互相打架。我最享受的，是在這團混亂裡找出背後的規則——然後成為定義那套規則的人。我的工作，就是把這些東西整理到讓人一看就懂、用得順手——而且這幾年越來越常是親手打造設計團隊自己拿來規劃的工具。
